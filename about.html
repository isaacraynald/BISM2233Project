About Us

Our Mission

We provide a healthy and balanced dining experience with the family from the comfort of your own home and to offer income to those who have any level of culinary skill to bring to the table!



Who are We?

Zuber Cooks ia a platform for people who whishes to obtain home-cooked meals for a large group or famillies who doesn't have the time to cook for themselves. For your convenience, our cooks are able to get the groceries for efficiency, and will cook right at your kitchen where you can watch to make sure you know what you are eating. You can also join our website to apply to be a cook for a side job, all you need is basic experience in cooking, and we will be assessing your skills to be given a lisence. So why not join now!
